Brief History

The Bandipora District has faced severe economic, social and administrative debacles due to the ongoing spate of terrorism in the state of Jammu and Kashmir since 1989. Further, its close proximity to borders and lack of basic amenities for a healthy civic life has compounded the problems intrinsically. One such sphere which merits immediate care and attention is education. While the State Government has elaborate plans in this regard, the implementation process is going to be slow and tedious. AGS, Baraub was started under aegis of Indian Army Sadhbhavana for the children of NBC Sector (J&K) as a good will gesture and as part of changing the perception of local public towards the larger prospects of national integration. At the time of opening of school, there were only 55 students. Gradually, the school became popular in the locality and the number of students started increasing. Subsequently a class each was added every year and till date school has extended upto class V. The school is being run with whole hearted support of the local public defying the threat from the terrorists at the time of even peak of militancy in J&K. The school has been recognized by the J&K government. The school is very effective in achieving the aim of the Army and the nation in winning the heart and mind of the local public of J&K. Army Goodwill School, Baraub established under Sadhbhavana project as founded on 14 Jan 2010. Since then, it is being managed by the unit deployed at Baraub and has established itself as a premiere institution amongst local inhabitants. The school has been run under the aegis of NBC Battalion to provide quality education to the children at a reasonable fee. The school is of primary standard affiliated to the J&K state board of education.

Admission

The Army Goodwill School is open for admission to children of all persons residing in Tilail Valley.

Syllabus

AGS is following syllabus of J&K BOSE.

Examination

In each academic year the school is holding the following tests and examinations:-

(a) Unit Test-2

(b) Half Yearly Examination.

(c) Annual Examination.

Fee Structure

The students of AGS, Baraub are paid following fees of the school.

(a) Admission Fee - Rs 100/- one time

(b) Tuition Fee - Rs 70/- pm. (only 10 month’s fee)
